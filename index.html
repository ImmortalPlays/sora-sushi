Our Story

A quiet devotion to the craft

Sora Sushi began with a simple belief: that great sushi is equal parts patience, respect for the ingredient, and the calm of a steady hand. Our chefs train for years before they ever stand at our counter.

We source our fish daily and shape every piece by hand. Sit at the counter and watch the craft up close, or settle into a quiet table — either way, you're our guest.
